About Us

Think India is a pan initiative that aims to bring together the finest talents of our country and foster a sense of 'Nation First' attitude. Our objective is to cultivate a nationalistic spirit and provide a forum for the "Leaders of Tomorrow" to engage in discussions on matters of national importance and propose innovative solutions to the challenges faced by India.

Think India was established in 2006 by students from esteemed institutions such as IISc, IIMB, NIMHANS, and NLSIU. The official inauguration of this forum took place at the Art of Living Ashram in Bengaluru in 2007.

We inculcate the collaboration of talented individuals from renowned institutes, all driven by a shared goal of contributing to the progress of our nation. Think India provides a platform for these bright minds to channel their passion and ideas towards the betterment of our society.
